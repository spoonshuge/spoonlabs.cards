Welcome to spoonLabs!

The card “game” is a rough place filled with the nicest people. I think I’ve been into baseball cards for about six months now—hard to say, since I don’t keep records (which is part of the problem). As best as I can tell, it’s a lot like poker: you’ve got your sharks grinding it out, the new kids with their apps and flipping strategies (repacks, WhatNot), a handful of collectors, a ton of Pokémon, and of course, the house. And speaking of the house—it always wins. Fanatics is raking. Anyway, cards were literal junk when I was a kid—but we didn’t know that then. The excitement was real when I pulled that Maddux Upper Deck SPx on Christmas morning. So beyond needing a hobby and always thinking I’ll strike it rich, there’s a bit of nostalgia here too. In the last six months, I’ve dropped way too much money on wax and breaks, hit up 10+ card shows, sold on eBay and WhatNot, sent cards to be graded (both myself and through a shop), and kept zero records. I know I already said that, but seriously—I need to fix that. Come along for the ride. And for the love of God, buy something from me. Peace, spoon
