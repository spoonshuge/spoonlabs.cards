Welcome to my little mess of a lab, where ideas in tech, sports, trends, and a healthy dose of gambling come together to spin. Okay, maybe not so healthy—I’ve spent way too much money on wax and breaks. But hey, it’s all in the name of passion, right?

As a proud child of the "junk card era," I carry a touch of nostalgia (and maybe a little regret) for the millions of cards that fueled my obsession. spoonLabs.cards is my way of embracing the chaos, sharing the thrill, and connecting with collectors like you who get it.
